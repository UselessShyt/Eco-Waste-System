Welcome, Guest! Learn About Recycling

Recycling is an essential practice that helps reduce waste, conserve resources, and protect our environment. By recycling, we can help minimize pollution and make better use of the materials we consume.

Top Tips for Recycling and Reducing Waste:

  • Separate your recyclables: paper, plastic, metal, and glass.
  • Always clean and dry recyclables before disposing of them.
  • Reduce single-use plastics by using reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  • Compost organic waste to create natural fertilizer for your garden.
  • Repurpose items like old clothes, electronics, and furniture instead of throwing them away.
